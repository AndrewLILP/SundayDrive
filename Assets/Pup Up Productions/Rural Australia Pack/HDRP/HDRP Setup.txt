HDRP setup for Demo scene:

Drag and drop Sky and Fog Global Volume into scene. 
Replace Directional Light in scene with the HDRP Directional Light prefab.
Under Lighting>Scene, drag Lighting Settings into Lighting Setting Asset slot.
Under Lighting tab, drag the Sky and Fog Settings Profile from the Demo Scene folder into the Profile slot.

Note: Included billboard grass is not supported in HDRP, so grass will not display. 
Please replace the grass with mesh grass of your liking.
